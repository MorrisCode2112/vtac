About vTAC

Virtual Tactical Air Command (vTAC) is a dedicated virtual USAF organization operating within Microsoft Flight Simulator and the VATSIM network. Our mission is to simulate realistic military aviation operations, focusing on pilot training and operations to achieve military VATSIM certifications. Established to foster teamwork, precision, and professionalism, vTAC offers members a unique opportunity to engage in immersive flight simulation with a military focus.

We are proud to be part of the VATSIM community, which connects over 140,000 active members worldwide, providing a realistic and engaging environment for virtual pilots and controllers.

Command Structure

Our command structure ensures disciplined and organized operations within vTAC. Below is the structure of our leaders driving the mission:

  • Senior Executive Service - Seniors of vTAC aimed to maintain synergy and mission effectiveness across all parts of vTAC.
  • Commanding General - Leads pilot and controller training programs for VATSIM certifications and commands overall operations.
  • Adjutant General - Manages administrative functions and provides support for various vTAC initiatives.

MAJCOMS

Majcom commanders lead the virual Major Commands. Their job is to lead, organize, train, and equip forces to execute mission objectives, ensuring readiness and operational success across the command.

  • Air Combat Command - Deliver dominant airpower to secure the nation and defeat adversaries.
  • Air Mobility Command - Provide rapid, global mobility and sustainment for America's armed forces.
  • Global Strike Command - Deter, assure, and strike globally with strategic nuclear and conventional forces.
